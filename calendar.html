You can link your bookings and / or availability to your personal calender by subscribing to one of these feeds. This means that all of your We Are Movers bookings will automatically pull through to your personal calendar. You only have to sunscribe once; any changes made will be reflected in your personal calendar.

Although the calendar updates instantly, it may take up to 24 hours for new bookings to show in your personal calendar depending on which type of calendar you are using.

You can use one of these links to add the feed to your calendar app, depending on whether you'd like to see your bookings, availability or both.
