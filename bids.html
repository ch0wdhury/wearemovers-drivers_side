Bid Status Key

ACTIVE Your bid has been sent to the customer and they are considering it.

REJECTED Unfortunately the customer has rejected your bid.

ACCEPTED The customer has accepted your bid and the auction has closed. This is not a booking until the customer has made a payment.

RETRACTED You have retracted your bid and the customer can no longer consider it.

NULLIFIED The bid has been cleared and won't count against your total bids for that job.

UNAVAILABLE You have another booking, or your calendar is showing you as unavailable at the time of this move. If you cannot make it you should retract your bid.
