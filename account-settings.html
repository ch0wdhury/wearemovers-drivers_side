Cash Only Payments

Cash only payments allow your customers to pay the full quote total in cash to you on the competition of the job. We Are Movers will take our commission from your account balance, allowing you to accept cash for the full quoted amount.

It will not affect the payment methods you currently are using, you will still be able to accept full card and part card/part cash payments.

The customer will only be able to make a full cash payment if you enable it, otherwise they won't be offered the opinion when they select your quote.
